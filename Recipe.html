Alfredo Penne

Ingredients:

1 pound (450g) penne pasta
1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
2 cups heavy cream
2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and black pepper, to taste
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1. Cook the Penne: Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.
2. Make the Alfredo Sauce: In a large skillet or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer.
3. Add Parmesan: Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the simmering cream, stirring continuously to ensure it melts evenly. Continue stirring until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.
4. Season the Sauce: Season the Alfredo sauce with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder to taste. Remember that Parmesan is salty, so adjust the salt accordingly.
5. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked and drained penne pasta to the Alfredo sauce. Toss the pasta gently until it is well coated with the sauce.
6. Garnish: If desired, garnish the Alfredo Penne with chopped fresh parsley for a burst of color and added flavor.
7. Serve: Serve the Alfredo Penne hot, and optionally, you can sprinkle extra Parmesan cheese on top when serving.
